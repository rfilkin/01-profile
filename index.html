About Me

Hello! I'm Robert Filkin. I graduated from UC Irvine with a double major in Computer Science and Computer Game Science. I'm currently attending a web design course at UCLA Extension, in hopes of learning some valuable industry skills.
Computers have always fascinated me. Programming, and by extension web development, often amounts to solving a puzzle of sorts - and when everything clicks into place, you've also got a nice functioning machine! It's great fun.
